What we do

RobotVacuumLab helps you make a good choice quickly. We summarize what matters, compare real trade-offs, and point out the “gotchas” that create regret: unreliable maps, hair tangles, weak edge behavior, loud stations, or apps that break routines.

What we don’t do

We don’t write hype. We don’t accept payment for positive coverage. When a pick is a close call, we say so. If we use affiliate links, it’s disclosed and does not change our recommendations.
