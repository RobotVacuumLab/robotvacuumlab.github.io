Quick FAQ

Buyer questions
Is mopping worth paying extra for?
For light daily dust and footprints: yes, it can help. For sticky spills: expectations matter. We prioritize systems that avoid over-wetting and don’t create extra hassle.
Do I need an auto-empty station?
If you run the robot often (or have pets), auto-empty can reduce weekly friction. The trade-off is noise during emptying and higher upfront cost.
How often should I do maintenance?
Most homes: quick brush + bin check weekly, filter checks every couple weeks. Pet homes and carpets usually need more frequent brush attention.
