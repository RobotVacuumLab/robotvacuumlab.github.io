FAQ

Is it worth paying extra for the dock?
If you run the robot frequently, the dock is the “friction reducer” that keeps your routine consistent. If you don’t mind emptying manually, value picks may be smarter.
Does it replace a full vacuum?
For daily upkeep, it can reduce how often you deep-clean. But most homes still benefit from occasional manual vacuuming, especially on carpets and edges.
