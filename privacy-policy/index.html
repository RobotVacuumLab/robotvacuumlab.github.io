Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. If we change something material, we update this page and the “Last updated” date.

Last updated: 2026-02-22

Overview

RobotVacuumLab is an editorial site. We aim to keep data collection minimal and use information only to operate the site, measure aggregated usage (if you allow analytics), and improve content quality.

Information we collect

  • Server/hosting logs: may include IP address, device/browser type, pages requested, and timestamps. Used for security and troubleshooting.
  • Analytics (optional): if you accept cookies, we may collect aggregated usage signals (e.g., pages viewed, approximate location at a broad level, device type) to understand what content is helpful.
  • Voluntary information: if you contact us, you may provide your name, email, and the message content.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use a cookie preference choice (accept/reject) to remember your selection. If you accept, analytics cookies may be set. You can also control cookies in your browser settings.

Affiliate links

Some pages contain affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not affect our recommendations. See Disclosure for details.

Third-party links

We link to third-party websites (retailers, manufacturers, and sources). Their privacy practices may differ. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

How we use information

  • Operate and secure the site (fraud prevention, abuse detection, debugging).
  • Measure content performance in aggregate (only if you accept analytics).
  • Respond to messages you send us.

Data retention

We keep server logs for a limited period for security and troubleshooting, then delete or anonymize where feasible. Contact messages are retained as long as needed to respond and maintain records.

Your choices

  • Analytics: use the cookie banner to accept or reject analytics cookies.
  • Browser controls: disable or clear cookies via your browser settings.
  • Affiliate tracking: you can avoid affiliate tracking by not clicking affiliate links.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Use the contact details on our About page.

Informational only

This policy is provided for general information and may not reflect every legal requirement in every jurisdiction. If you need legal advice, consult a qualified professional.
